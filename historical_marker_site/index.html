Project mission

Why Krewe TU History exists

Krewe TU History is an interactive digital history project connecting Tulane University and New Orleans to the wider world. Through short stories, quick reads, and community voices, we make local history visible.

By surfacing global connections and underrepresented perspectives, we learn more about the lived experiences of everyday people and cultures that have shaped this city and campus.

Our goal is to spark curiosity and dialogue across difference by meeting people where they already are, and inviting the Tulane community to engage with New Orleans as a living, globally connected space.

This project is built and maintained by members of the 2025-2026 Cohort the Intercultural Leadership Program.

Some story content and "Did You Know" facts featured on this site were drafted with AI assistance, using sources and references provided by contributors. All content is grounded in cited materials, and the histories and perspectives they represent belong to the people and communities who shared them.
